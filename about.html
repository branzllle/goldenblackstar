Our
making process

#1

Crushing Process

Both coconut and nut shells are thoroughly cleaned and crushed into fine particles using advanced crushing machinery. This ensures a uniform texture, preparing the material for the next stage.

#2

Mixing Process

The crushed coconut and nut shell particles are mixed together in precise proportions. This blend combines the unique qualities of both materials, creating a balanced composition for superior performance.

#3

Blending Process

A natural, chemical-free binder is added to the mixture. The blend is thoroughly kneaded to ensure uniformity, resulting in a strong, cohesive base for the briquettes.

#4

Molding Process

The blended mixture is pressed into molds using high-pressure machines. Briquettes are crafted into various shapes like cubes or hexagons, suitable for BBQ and Shisha applications.

#5

Drying Process

The molded briquettes are transferred to drying facilities, where they undergo either sun-drying or oven-drying. This process removes all moisture, ensuring the briquettes are lightweight, durable, and ready for use.

#6

Packing Process

The finished briquettes are carefully packed into eco-friendly, customizable packaging. Each package is designed to protect the product during transport and reflect our commitment to quality and sustainability.

#7

Why Both Coconut & Nut Shells?

By utilizing both coconut and nut shells, we create a unique charcoal blend that combines the high heat and clean burn of coconut shells with the dense, long-lasting properties of nut shells. This ensures a premium product that meets the needs of BBQ and Shisha enthusiasts while promoting sustainable resource use.
